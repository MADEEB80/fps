Welcome to
VANGUARD PUBLIC SCHOOL & COLLEGE

Established in 1981, VANGUARD PUBLIC SCHOOL & COLLEGE is a private O and A Level institution with the prime objective of providing quality education. Today, VFPS is one of the leading private schools in the country with state-of-the-art campuses, innovative pedagogical methods, outstanding academic achievements, exceptional university placement, and an extensive global alumni network.
