Jauhar Campus

FPS is renowned for its state-of-the- art facilities and the Jauhar campus is no exception. Nested in the heart of Jauhar, the FPS Jauhar campus offers the following facilities:

‣ State of the Art Design
‣ Learning Centers
‣ Educational Technology Resources
‣ Air Conditioned Classrooms
‣ Outdoor Play
‣ Trained teaching faculty
‣ Extracurricular activities
‣ Learning Communities
